About HOSKBREW

Preserving Gaming Legacy Through Professional Excellence

Our Mission

At HOSKBREW, we are dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich legacy of vintage gaming. Our mission is to be the ultimate destination for all things retro—whether it's offering classic consoles, games, custom-made parts, or expert repair services.

We believe in empowering our customers by providing everything they need to restore and maintain their systems, ensuring that vintage gaming remains accessible for years to come.

Right to Repair

As passionate advocates for the right to repair, we're proud to provide not only high-quality parts but also repair manuals, schematics, and guidance to help our customers repair their own consoles.

Whether you're tackling a simple fix or a complex restoration project, HOSKBREW believes that the power to repair should be in the hands of the gamer.

Innovation & Craftsmanship

In addition to our extensive selection of products and repair services, we also create retro games, combining the nostalgic charm of retro gameplay with modern innovation.

Our commitment to craftsmanship extends to every part we create, ensuring top-quality components and systems that keep your gaming experience smooth.

Community Focus

At HOSKBREW, we're more than just a business—we're a community. Our online forum provides a space for enthusiasts to connect, share knowledge, and support each other in their retro gaming journeys.

From troubleshooting advice to game recommendations, we're here to help our members thrive.
